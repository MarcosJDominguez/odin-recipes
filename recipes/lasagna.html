Lasagna: Like a Casserole with Pizza Flavor

Some big ol LASAGNA!

Lasagna: The Midnight Snack of Champions

When I was a boy, I envied Garfield the cartoon cat. Why? Because he had the freedom to eat lasagna whenever he wanted.

With this recipe in hand, we can experience this freedom. Bon apetite.

Ingredients

  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Garlic (powdered or fresh, whatever you can get your hands on)
  • Basil
  • Trader Joe's Italian Seasoning Blend
  • Hope and a longing to tap dance on the periphery of the Milky Way
  • Ground Beef, extra fatty
  • Lasagna pasta sheets, the kind with the curly ends, you know?

Steps

  1. Mix that beef, TJ seasoning, garlic, basil, and the hope and longing in a deep bowl
  2. Set your oven to something real hot, 350 degrees fahrenheit
  3. Place all the meat mixin on the lasagna sheets, over a pan
  4. Cover the pan, throw it in the oven
  5. Eyeball the lasanga until it's done, serve when it pleases you
