Sumptuous Seafood Sausage Risotto

Regal Risotto, with Fancy Sausage and Extravagant Seafood

Perfect date night meal

Risotto, the perfect dish to impress a stranger

A meal I learned to make to myself look like a real cook,

this is the dish to break out when looking to make an impression.

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice
  • Garlic, oregano, salt
  • Shrimp
  • Scallops
  • Sweet Italian Sausage
  • Trader Joe's Italian Seasoning
  • Lot more love
  • Soup stock
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the rice in the soup stock along with all the herbs
  2. Cook shrimp and scallops in olive oil and crushed garlic in separate pan
  3. Chop up and fry the sausage in another pan in olive oil
  4. Throw shrimp, scallops, and sausage into the rice soup and mix
  5. Serve it to your loved ones
